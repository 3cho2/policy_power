> [!warning]
> No hate/extremism, no harassment, no targeting protected classes.

## 1) Allowed model
- Allowed via whitelist approval.
- Orgs must maintain leadership + roster + escalation approach.

## 2) Approval requirements
- Apply: [[Templates/Template - Criminal Org Application\|Templates/Template - Criminal Org Application]]
- Staff review: Owner/Admin decision
- Maintain roster: [[Templates/Template - Criminal Org Roster\|Templates/Template - Criminal Org Roster]]

## 3) Hard restrictions (not allowed)
- Hate/extremist groups/symbolism
- Doxxing/harassment/OOC coercion
- Exploits/bug abuse
- “Unstoppable” RP (must be catchable/investigatable)

## 4) RP fairness rules
- Value life and pace escalation
- Consequences apply (arrests/losses)
- No “always win” scenes

## 7) Events and story arcs
- Request arcs via: [[01 HQ/Community/03 - Suggestions & Feedback\|01 HQ/Community/03 - Suggestions & Feedback]]
- Major arcs should produce an AAR summary if they impact server policy/training/dev.

## 8) Enforcement
- Warnings → suspension → disbanding → bans depending on severity
- Appeals follow staff process.

## 9) Approval status & 7-day expiration
> [!warning]
> Approvals expire after 7 days of inactivity unless extended by Lambo/Admin.

### What counts as activity
At least one within 7 days:
- Org RP event/scene summary
- Org meeting note
- Staff-approved arc update
- Roster update tied to in-server action

Log activity:
- [[01 HQ/Community/10 - Criminal Org Activity Log (Index)\|01 HQ/Community/10 - Criminal Org Activity Log (Index)]]
- Or per-org activity log note using template

### Expiration effect
- No recruiting/expansion
- No org-flagged operations/arcs
- Individuals can still RP as CIV without claiming org authority

### Renewal
- Update roster + post check-in + request reactivation
- Approved by Org owner proposal + staff approval OR Lambo/Admin
