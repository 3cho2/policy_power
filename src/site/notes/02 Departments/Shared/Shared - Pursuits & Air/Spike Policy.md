## Pursuit ownership (Policy Power)
> [!note]
> **Lead/IC stays with the initiating jurisdiction** until SAHP arrives and a transfer is completed (Option 1).

### Transfer of pursuit lead
1. “`SAHP <Unit> on pursuit, requesting lead.`”
2. “`Copy. Transferring lead to SAHP <Unit>.`”
3. “`SAHP <Unit> has lead.`”
4. Dispatch updates lead + comms plan.

## Pursuit Supervisor
- On-duty supervisor of lead agency
- If none: highest-ranking unit is temporary supervisor

## Tactics authorization (Supervisor Approval Required)
> [!warning]
> **PIT and Spikes require Supervisor Approval** before deployment.

**PIT**
- Lead: “`Request PIT authorization.`”
- Supervisor: “`Approved/Denied.`”

**Spikes**
- Lead: “`Request spikes authorization at <location>.`”
- Supervisor: “`Approved/Denied.`”
- If approved: confirm placement + retrieval plan + safety.

## If supervisor is unavailable
- No PIT/spikes unless imminent threat to life; document in AAR/supp notes.
