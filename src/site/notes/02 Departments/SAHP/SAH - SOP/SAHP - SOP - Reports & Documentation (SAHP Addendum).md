# SAHP - SOP - Reports & Documentation (SAHP Addendum)

## 1) Case number (required)
`PP-YYYYMMDD-SAHP-####` where `####` is continuous per SAHP.

## 2) Report quality minimum
Every report should include:
- What happened (clear narrative)
- Who was involved
- Where/when
- What actions were taken
- Outcome/charges (if any)
- Evidence reference (if any)

## 3) Supplements
If other units assisted (transport, booking, evidence):
- Ensure supplemental notes exist and are linked to the case number.
